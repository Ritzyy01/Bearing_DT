Wind Turbine Bearing Digital Twin

Real-time Data Stream & PCA Health Indicator Synthesis

Model Architecture & Feature Engineering

1. Feature Extraction: The incoming signal is processed to extract RMS, Kurtosis, and Crest Factor. A Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) isolates fault frequencies.
2. Feature Normalization: A pre-computation pass scales PCA variance, mapping the Health Indicator dynamically to a 1.0 (Healthy) to 0.0 (Failed) bounds.
3. Health Indicator (HI) Creation: Selected features are fused using PCA. An Exponential Moving Average (EMA) filter is applied to smooth out environmental noise and reveal the true degradation trend.
